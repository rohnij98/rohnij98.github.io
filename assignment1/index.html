12) What is the most frustrating part of the interaction to you and what makes that part frustrating?

One of the most frustrating parts of interacting with a website is when there is too much clutter on the page, and there is no clear flow guiding the user to their end product. For example, if the user is on a shopping site and is looking for something in particular, then having too many ads and not a clear layout harms the user experience as the user will find it frustrating to search for the product they are looking for and won’t be keen to return to your site.
